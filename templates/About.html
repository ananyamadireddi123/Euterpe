Welcome to Euterpe, a music-focused website created by Ananya and Nishita, two dedicated CSE students at IIIT-H. Our goal is to provide a platform that showcases the top artists and their most popular songs based on reviews, creating an IMDb equivalent for music enthusiasts.
As a team of two, we have put a significant amount of effort into designing and developing this website. Our passion for music and commitment to creating an exceptional user experience has driven us to create a site that not only looks beautiful but also provides valuable insights into the music industry.
Allow us to introduce ourselves. Ananya Madireddy, an 18-year-old embodiment of determination and genuine kindness, navigates through challenges with grace and enthusiasm. motivating those around her to embrace a more upbeat perspective on life. Nishita Kannan, also 18 years old, complements Ananya's strengths with her organizational skills and passion for engaging conversations. Her attention to detail guarantees our site's content is beautifully structured, while her amiable nature makes collaborating with her a pleasure.
We hope you enjoy exploring Euterpe and discovering new music. As we continue to develop this project, we will update our About page with new information, so stay tuned!
